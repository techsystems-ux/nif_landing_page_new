Frequently Asked Questions

What educational qualification is mandatory for admission at NIF Pune Deccan ?

Admission to NIF Pune Deccan requires at least a 10th or 12th grade for certificate and diploma courses, 12th grade for undergraduate courses (B.Voc, B.Des, BBA), and a bachelor's degree for postgraduate courses (M.Voc). Always verify with the institution for the latest details.

Do the courses at NIF Pune Deccan provide industry exposure to the students ?

Yes, the courses at NIF Pune Deccan provide industry exposure to students. The institute offers internships, workshops, live projects, and collaborations with industry professionals, helping students gain practical experience and connect with the fashion and design industry.

Does NIF Pune Deccan provide internships ?

Yes. Students get structured internship placements in their 2nd and 3rd year with leading brands. Past students have interned at Tommy Hilfiger, Myntra, and Aditya Birla Fashion. Over 85% of our graduates are placed within 6 months of completing their course.

Will the faculty members at NIF Pune Deccan help me build my portfolio ?

Yes, the faculty members at NIF Pune Deccan actively assist students in building their portfolios. They provide guidance and mentorship, helping students showcase their skills through projects, assignments, and portfolio development, which is crucial for their future career in the fashion and design industry.

How can I get more information about NIF Pune Deccan ?

To get more information about NIF Pune Deccan, you can visit their official website for details on courses and admissions, contact the admissions office for specific queries, follow their social media for updates, or attend webinars and campus visits to interact with faculty and students.
